Install SignagePanel
on your Fire Stick

Follow these simple steps to get your digital signage screen up and running in under 10 minutes.

📦

SignagePanel Player

Version 1.3 · Android APK · ~5MB

⬇️ Download APK
For Amazon Fire Stick (2nd gen or newer) · Android TV devices
Step by step

How to install

1

Enable "Apps from Unknown Sources"

On your Fire Stick, go to Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options and turn on Apps from Unknown Sources.

⚙️ If you don't see Developer Options, go to My Fire TV → About and click the Fire TV name 7 times to unlock it.
2

Install the Downloader app

Search for "Downloader" in the Fire Stick app store and install it. It's free and lets you install APK files.

3

Download the SignagePanel APK

Open Downloader and enter this URL in the address bar:

https://pub-dfe4494897fd44f180ebedc0a3dc7786.r2.dev/app-release.apk
Tap the URL above to copy it
4

Install and open the app

Once downloaded, tap Install then Open. The SignagePanel Player will launch and show a QR code on screen.

5

Activate your screen

On your phone or computer, go to app.signagepanel.app, sign in to your account, and go to Activate Screen. Scan the QR code shown on the TV — your screen is now live.

🎉 That's it! You can now assign playlists and upload content from your dashboard.
